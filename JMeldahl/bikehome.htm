Welcome to Lyle's Bicycle Shop

Conveniently located on the scenic Root River Trail, one of southeast Minnesota's most beautiful bike trails, Lyle's Bicycle Shop offers a variety of services ranging from repair to brand-new, top-quality bicycles. The staff are knowledgeable and passionate about their craft. What started out as a hobby 50 years ago has turned into a growing family business, which Lyle now operates with his children, grandchildren, and great-grandchildren.

We service all makes and models of bicycles and our dedicated staff will gladly help with any of your cycling needs.

Our History

Handy with a wrench and eager to help, Lyle Lee started out fixing bicycles for friends and family as a hobby over 50 years ago. After moving near the Root River Trail in Peterson, MN, Lyle was able to extend a helping hand to bicyclists and word soon spread about the "Bike Man" in Peterson. People come from miles around to have their bicycles fixed with care or to purchase a new bicycle that will last for years to come.

As his business grew, his family joined in. Children, grandchildren, and even great-grandchildren help in all aspects of the company. Sons, Ardell, Steve, and Jeff, assist with bicycle repairs and maintenance. His daughter, Joanne, keeps daily operations running smoothly and heads the accounting department. Her husband, Keith, a retired Marine, acquires vintage or rare items. Grandson, Jeremy, manages the Information Technology and Communications departments; granddaughter, Jaime, is head of sales and purchasing; and granddaughter, Jessica, manages graphic and web design while her husband, David, perfects custom paint jobs. Great-grandchildren, Jordan and Kingston, keep everyone on their toes, greet customers, and provide plenty of laughter and fun!
